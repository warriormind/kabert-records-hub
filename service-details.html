Records & Document Management

Comprehensive digital and physical records management including correspondence tracking, filing, scanning, indexing, and secure archiving solutions for organizations across Africa.

At Kabert Records Hub Limited, we provide end-to-end records management solutions that ensure compliance with legal requirements while improving operational efficiency. Our services include digital document scanning, secure archiving, correspondence tracking, and workflow automation to help organizations transition to paperless operations.

What's Included

Document Digitization

Professional scanning and conversion of physical documents to digital formats with OCR technology.

Correspondence Tracking

Complete tracking system for incoming and outgoing correspondence with automated notifications.

Secure Archiving

Cloud-based secure storage with encryption and access controls for sensitive documents.

Compliance Management

Ensuring all records management practices comply with local and international regulations.
